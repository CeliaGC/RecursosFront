What is this?

We are community of developers training ourselves in bot player designs. Each of us own an API through which our bots can play games with our colleague's.

Our goal is to get step by step to master harder, more complex and more interesting games. Here you will be able to learn with tutorials and examples, to interact with the community, to participate in events and to certificate your achievements.

If you come on behalf of a company or institution and are interested in testing our community with a specific challenge, please send your requirements to asd@asd.com.
